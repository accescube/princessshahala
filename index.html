About Us

At Princess Shahala Furniture, we believe that furniture is more than just a utility—it's an expression of comfort and art. Based in the heart of Mangalore, we specialize in premium, handcrafted cane wood furniture that brings the warmth of nature into your home.

Our artisans combine traditional weaving techniques with modern design aesthetics, ensuring each piece is unique, sustainable, and built to last for generations. From our iconic rounded sofa sets to custom wall units, we are committed to elegance in every weave and comfort in every curve.
